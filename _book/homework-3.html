#> [1] 0.977

4.1.2 P(x > 55)

\[P(x > 55) = P (z >\frac{55 - 36}{3})\]

P(x > 55) = P(z > 6.333) = 0

pnorm(55, m=36, s=3, lower.tail = FALSE)
#> [1] 1.2e-10

Since the probability is extremly low, the conclusion is that the travel voucher was misplaced or lost.
